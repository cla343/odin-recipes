Steak



Perfectly cooked steak

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • New York Steak 10oz
  • Steak Seasoning of Your Choice

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees
  2. Cook on one side for 8 minutes then flip. Cook for an additional 6 minutes
  3. Now, over high heat sear your steak for 30 seconds per side with 2 tbsp of butter in the skillet. Let cook and enjoy!
